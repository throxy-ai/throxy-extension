# Pockla - Cold Calling Guide

## What Pockla Does (Read This First)

Pockla helps companies make money from consumer databases they already have.

Many companies in the UK have large lists of people who signed up for something — energy quotes, insurance comparisons, cashback offers, prize draws. Those people gave permission to be contacted by partner companies. But most of the time, those lists just sit there.

Pockla takes those lists and runs personalised outreach — emails and landing pages built specifically for each person (their location, their situation, their needs). This turns dormant contacts into qualified leads. Pockla then sells those leads to companies that need customers (installers, insurers, solicitors, etc.) and splits the revenue with the list owner.

**In one sentence:** "We turn your opted-in consumer list into revenue — we handle everything, you get half."

## Who You're Calling

There are three types of companies you'll be calling. The conversation is different for each.

### Type 1: List Partners (Top Priority)

**What they are:** Companies that OWN a large opted-in consumer database. They collected these contacts through their own website, product, or service.

**Examples:** Lead generation companies, comparison websites, cashback platforms, membership organisations, publishers, survey/panel companies.

**What we offer them:** "Give us your list. We run the outreach. We sell the leads. You get 50% of the revenue. No work from your side."

**How to spot them:** They talk about "our database", "our opted-in contacts", "our subscribers", "our members". They have a website where consumers sign up for something.

### Type 2: Lead Buyers

**What they are:** Companies that BUY leads to service customers. They need a steady flow of people who want their product.

**Examples:** Solar panel installers, boiler companies, insurance brokers, solicitors, home improvement firms.

**What we offer them:** "We deliver qualified, compliant leads at a fixed cost per lead. You only pay for leads that meet your criteria."

**How to spot them:** They talk about "lead volume", "cost per lead", "lead quality", "our pipeline". They already buy leads from other sources.

### Type 3: Tech Partners

**What they are:** Lead generation companies that already have their own funnels and buyers. They want better technology to improve conversion.

**Examples:** Established lead gen agencies that run their own campaigns but want to lift conversion rates.

**What we offer them:** "Keep your funnels and your buyers. We add our personalisation stack on top — you pay a retainer plus a share of the extra revenue we generate."

**How to spot them:** They talk about "our funnels", "our conversion rates", "our buyers". They already run campaigns but want better results.

## The Problem

### For List Partners / Lead Gen Founders

{#990000}**"The lead gen firms and list owners we talk to usually tell us:"**{/}

- They're sitting on tens or hundreds of thousands of opted-in contacts, but most of those contacts are dormant — not generating any revenue
- When they do email their list, it's generic blasts that get low open rates and low conversion
- They know the data is valuable, but they don't have the tech or the team to personalise outreach at scale
- Building landing pages for different segments requires dev time they don't have
- They want to monetise the list further but don't want to hire more people or buy more tools

### For Lead Buyers

{#990000}**"The companies we supply leads to usually tell us:"**{/}

- Lead quality from their current sources is inconsistent — too many tyre-kickers, not enough genuine interest
- Cost per lead keeps going up, especially on paid channels like Google and Meta
- They need a steady, predictable volume of leads that actually convert into customers
- Compliance is a concern — especially in regulated industries like energy schemes, insurance, and claims

### For Tech Partners

{#990000}**"The lead gen firms we partner with usually tell us:"**{/}

- Their conversion rates have plateaued and they can't squeeze more out of their existing funnels
- Every visitor and every lead sees the same generic pages, regardless of who they are
- Personalising pages for different segments requires dev work they can't prioritise
- Their buyers are demanding better lead quality, and differentiation is getting harder

## Discovery Questions

*{#999999}Ask these naturally during the conversation. React to their answer before asking the next one.{/}*

### Must Ask (All Calls)

{#990000}**1. "Do you have an opted-in consumer database?"**{/}
   - Yes → "Roughly how many contacts are we talking?" (30k+ is ideal)
  - *{#999999}No → Check if they're a lead buyer instead. If neither, DISQUALIFY.{/}*

{#990000}**2. "Are you a lead buyer, a lead generator, or both?"**{/}
   - Lead generator with a list → List partner conversation
   - Lead buyer → Lead buyer conversation
   - Both → Even better — both sides of the model apply

{#990000}**3. "What industries or verticals are you in?"**{/}
   - Energy/ECO4, solar, insurance, claims, home improvement, legal → Strong fit
  - *{#999999}B2B only → DISQUALIFY{/}*
   - Other B2C → Might work — probe further

### Nice to Ask

{#990000}**4. "How are you monetising your opted-in list today?"**{/}
   - Not doing much / generic blasts → Huge opportunity. "That's exactly where we come in."
   - Already monetising → "Good — we add on top of what you're already doing. The personalisation layer is what lifts conversion."

{#990000}**5. "What's your current conversion rate on outbound?"**{/}
   - Low / don't know → "That's normal. Most lists convert at 1-2%. Our personalisation lifts that significantly."
   - Decent → "Great baseline. We typically improve on that because every contact gets a personalised experience."

{#990000}**6. "Do you work in regulated verticals — FCA, energy schemes?"**{/}
   - Yes → Emphasise compliance: "We're compliance-first. Everything is consented, auditable, and built for regulated verticals."

## The Offer & Close

### Primary Offer

"Here's what I'd suggest — we run a 30-day pilot on a subset of your list. We handle the outreach, build personalised landing pages for each contact, and sell the leads. You see the conversion data and the revenue. If it works, we scale. If not, no harm done."

"Let us run a sample of 1,000 contacts from your list. You see the results. Zero risk, zero cost."

"We'll start supplying leads at an agreed CPL. You set the criteria — vertical, geography, qualification level. If the leads don't convert, we adjust. No long-term commitment needed upfront."

"Rupert would love to walk you through how it works — he can show you what the personalised outreach looks like for your actual contacts. 15 minutes this week?"
